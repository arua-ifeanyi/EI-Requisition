How do I get started with the EI-Requisition Software?

To get started, simply sign up for an account on our website, install the software (if needed), and follow the on-screen prompts to set up your store, inventory, and payment settings. You can also refer to the onboarding tutorial to guide you through the process.

Do I need any special hardware to use the software?

The software can be accessed on standard desktop or laptop computers, and some versions may work on tablets or mobile devices. Depending on your needs, you might also want to connect peripherals like barcode scanners or receipt printers, which are typically supported by the software.

How secure is my data?

We take data security seriously. All data is encrypted, and we follow best practices for secure storage and transmission. Additionally, the software complies with industry standards and regulations, such as GDPR and PCI DSS.

Can I assign roles and permissions to users?

Yes, you can assign different roles (e.g., cashier, manager, admin) with specific permissions to control access to different parts of the software. This helps ensure that only authorized users can make critical changes, such as inventory adjustments or financial reports.

Is there a free trial available?

We offer several pricing tiers to suit different business needs, including basic, standard, and premium plans. Each plan offers different levels of features and support, and you can upgrade or downgrade at any time.
